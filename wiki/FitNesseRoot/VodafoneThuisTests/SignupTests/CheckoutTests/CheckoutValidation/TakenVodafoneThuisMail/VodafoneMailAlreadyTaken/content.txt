Email addresses are reserved based on postcode/housenumber. Let's test '''we can't use the same email for orders on two different addresses.'''

!define TAKEN_MAIL_ADDRESS {vf_already_taken_test}
First order, no problem

|script              |vodafone choose browser test                                                       |
|go to packages                                                                                          |
|Open Package Details|100                                                                                |
|click               |Bestel direct                                                                      |
|enter               |${POSTAL_CODE_NO_500}         |as              |Postcode                           |
|enter               |${POSTAL_CODE_NO_500_HOUSE_NR}|as              |Huisnummer                         |
|click               |Verstuur                      |and wait for tag|h2|with text|Mogen wij je gegevens?|
|click               |Mevrouw                                                                            |
|enter               |T.                            |as              |Voornaam                           |
|enter               |Janssen                       |as              |Achternaam                         |
|enter               |email@test.nl                 |as              |E-mailadres                        |
|enter               |34528616                      |as              |Telefoonnummer mobiel              |
|enter               |27091975                      |as              |Geboortedatum                      |
|enter               |${TAKEN_MAIL_ADDRESS}         |as              |Nieuw Vodafone Thuis e-mailadres   |
